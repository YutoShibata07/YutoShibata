Yuto Shibata 柴田優斗

I am a first-year PhD student at Aoki lab in Keio University in Japan.

My specialties are computer vision, wireless sensing, and deep learning. Especially, I have a strong interest in building privacy-conscious ML systems, such as non-image-based human sensing.

I am primarily advised by Prof. Yoshimitsu Aoki and Dr. Mariko Isogawa.

I will be staying at Carnegie Mellon University for one year starting in June 2025, under the supervision of Prof. Katerina Fragkiadaki. There, I plan to conduct research on human motion and physics engines.

I'm currently working part-time job at Tenchijin and engaged in geospatial data analysis. Previously, I was doing research internship at Preferred Networks where I conducted research on communication-efficient federated learning in healthcare.

Research opportunities: I am looking for research internships in Japan or abroad. I am also happy to answer questions about my research. If you are interested, please send me an email.
